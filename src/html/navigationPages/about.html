Akasha would be an information technology company with a focus on cloud computing, crypto-currency, computational linguistics, data representation, knowledge mapping, and artificial intelligence. Akasha ultimately aims to be the platform that could eventually give rise to the technological singularity, and to create a cryptocurrency whose value is derived from that. This website serves as a brief overview and introduction to Akasha and the ideas behind it.
